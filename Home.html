Letran Street Dance Crew is the official dance organization of Colegio de San Juan de Letran Calamba. The crew is composed of talented students from the different departments of the Colegio. With our dance, we entertain our audience and form camaraderie with our fellow Letranites. We live by the principles and values of a true Letranite.
Together, we share our talents and skills and show how Letranites battle on the dance floor.
